## Triage Guidelines

**High Priority**: Blocking functionality or user experience
**Medium Priority**: Important but not blocking release

**Status Values**:
- Open: New ticket, needs investigation
- In Progress: Being worked on
- Blocked: Waiting on dependencies
- Resolved: Fixed and tested
- Closed: Completed or won't fix
